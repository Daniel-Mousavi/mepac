Our Standard

What it takes to earn our endorsement.

01

Pro-growth on the record

A clear, demonstrated commitment to cutting red tape, opening capital, and letting Michigan build.

02

Open to everyone

A firm stand that no one is locked out of building a business here because of where they come from.

03

Accountable

A willingness to govern in the open and to be measured by their votes, not their press releases.

04

Viable

A real path to winning and to making a difference for Michigan’s economy once in office.

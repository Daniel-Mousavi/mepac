Who We Are

A coalition for a Michigan that works.

The Michigan Economic Empowerment PAC exists to give the people who power Michigan’s economy, small business owners, entrepreneurs, workers, and civic leaders, a real seat at the table.

Born out of Michigan’s diverse business and civic community, including its deep ties to the American Arab Chamber of Commerce, we are pro-growth, pro-trade, and unapologetically focused on opportunity. We believe the same rules should apply to everyone, that talent shouldn’t be wasted, and that a person’s origin should never decide whether they can build a business here.

We are not a think tank and not a trade group. We are a political committee built to move outcomes: who gets elected, how they vote, and what gets built.

Our Mission

To build political power and influence that advance inclusive economic growth, opportunity, and prosperity for all Michiganders.

Our vision: a Michigan where every community thrives in a global economy, and every voice has a seat at the table.

How We Work

Four levers, one goal.

We translate the priorities of Michigan’s builders into political results, in the open and on the record.

01

Endorse

We back candidates, of any party, who commit to a pro-growth, pro-opportunity agenda for Michigan.

02

Score

We track key votes and grade legislators, so voters and donors can see who delivered and who didn’t.

03

Publish

We put out clear research, briefs, and reports that make the case and hold decision-makers accountable.

04

Mobilize

We organize our base, business owners and community leaders, into a constituency politicians can’t ignore.

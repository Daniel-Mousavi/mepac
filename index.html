Leadership

The people accountable for every dollar.

A coalition of business, civic, and community leaders from across Michigan, accountable for how the committee operates and every dollar it spends.

Ahmad Chebbani
Founder & CEO, Omnex Accounting & Tax Services

An entrepreneur and civic leader with more than 35 years guiding Michigan businesses through complex financial systems. He founded Tejara to support new American entrepreneurs and recently led a Michigan trade delegation to the MENA region, opening new partnerships for the state.

Bilal Hammoud
CEO, Tejara

Appointed by Governor Gretchen Whitmer to Michigan’s Workforce Development Commission. He leads Tejara, an incubator for immigrant-owned businesses, and previously served in the Michigan Department of State. He holds degrees from Wayne State and certificates from Michigan State and Harvard Business School.

Ali Dagher
Attorney & Owner, Detroit Title & Escrow

A Michigan attorney with more than 35 years in the Detroit-area business community. He owns Detroit Title & Escrow, with offices in Detroit and Dearborn, and serves on the Wayne County Economic Development Board and the City of Dearborn Library Commission.

Ali Sayed
Founder & CEO, HYPE Athletics Community

Founder of HYPE Athletics Community, a nonprofit serving more than 52,000 youth and families each year through athletics, education, and wellness. Since 2001 he has grown it into one of Michigan’s most impactful community organizations, spanning over 290,000 square feet of space.
